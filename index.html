About

A systems‑oriented practitioner.

I help teams understand complex environments—where cultural dynamics, operational constraints, and sustainability goals meet. My background spans analytics, strategy, and cross‑cultural work across organizations and communities.

I’m especially interested in how seemingly small design decisions ripple through systems: how metrics shape behaviour, how tools reframe problems, and how everyday experiences can invite more inclusive, resilient futures.
